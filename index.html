more information

The Virtual Autopsy is software powered by the Unity3D game engine to aid universities and health education centers. Using the visible human projects datasets (VHP) and Volume rendering methods, our solution is the best way to reduce the unpleasant experience of using real corpses in autopsy. The preservation of real corpses requires expensive and toxic preservatives, using a virtual environment helps keeping the laboratory safe and clean and eliminates the extra costs of buying preservatives and real corpses.
